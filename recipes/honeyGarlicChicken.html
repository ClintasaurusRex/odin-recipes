This recipe for Honey Garlic Chicken Thighs offers a simple yet flavorful dish that can be prepared in a skillet. Boneless, skinless chicken thighs are seasoned and then seared until golden, after which a quick sauce made from honey, water, rice wine vinegar, and soy sauce is added to the pan with crushed garlic. The chicken simmers in this sweet and savory glaze until the sauce reduces and thickens, resulting in tender, sticky chicken thighs that are a crowd-pleasing option for a quick weeknight meal.

  • 8 (5 ounce) boneless chicken thighs
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed
  • ½ medium onion, finely chopped
  • 7 cloves garlic, chopped, or to taste
  • 1 cup honey
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 1 pinch onion powder, or to taste
  • 1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
